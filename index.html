inside col 2 : images here

A Full-Stack Express App

RSVPtree is an expressJS app with the intention of streamlining the RSVP process for big parties like weddings and family reunions. It uses sequelize to track the database and form information of various parties and keep them seperate. It focuses in on what information is needed from guests to better and more swiftly plan a party and keep track of what's going on. The cool part about it, besides how easy it is to use as a guest, is the family tree(s) it will help you make to learn family connections better - which comes in hand for weddings especially if the bride or groom doesn't know their better half's extended family super well.

Technologies used

  • Express.JS
  • Node.JS
  • Sequelize
  • Postgres
  • oAuth
  • HTML5/CSS3/Javascript
