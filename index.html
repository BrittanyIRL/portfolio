Hi, I'm Brittany. I'm a front end developer who delights in creative problem solvings, strong code, and out of the box thinking. I am #blessed to be doing what I love.

Full Stack is pretty broad, so here's a few things I can't get enough of.

HTML5 & CSS3

I believe in a strong foundation - which means HTML5 and CSS3. This is also the foundation that creates a responsive page, one of the most important aspects of a modern web page. I take pride in knowing my creations can be viewed on a variety of screens and sizes. Additionally, I have several years experience building sites with just these tools and still think they are fun to use - I consider it a personal challenge to center objects well.

Javascript & jQuery

Oh how boring the web would be without front end javascript and jquery! I love animating and beautifying pages with jQuery, as well as harnessing it to illustrate purposes and better organize page elements. These are broad and powerful tools at our disposal and not to be taken for granted.

Express.js

Express is great for building more databasae driven applications. I use this in conjuction with sequeilze, postgres, and node.js. EJS layouts in express make designing responsive and variable content sites smooth and sturdy.
